Builder Notes

  • Cyan squares on the ship are open mount points. Drop a part onto one to snap it in.
  • Hull 1x2 and 1x3 pieces extend straight out from the socket they connect to.
  • Press `R` while dragging blasters, thrusters, or shields to change their facing before you drop them.
  • Drag any mounted non-cockpit part off the ship to detach it, or right-click it for a quick detach.
