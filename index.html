How to Play

Steal Parts. Stay Alive.

Start as a bare cockpit, tear enemy ships apart, and rebuild faster than the next fight can overwhelm you.

Fly + Shoot

Use WASD to move and turn. Press Space to fire.

Rebuild Live

Mouse click/drag to attach/detach parts. Drag loose salvage onto cyan sockets to snap it in.

Place + Read

Press R to rotate the dragged part before you drop it. Press Q to cycle glow quality.

Builder Notes

  • Cyan squares on the ship are open mount points. Drop a part onto one to snap it in.
  • Hull 1x2 and 1x3 pieces extend straight out from the socket they connect to.
  • Press `R` while dragging blasters, thrusters, or shields to change their facing before you drop them.
  • Drag any mounted non-cockpit part off the ship to detach it, or right-click it for a quick detach.
